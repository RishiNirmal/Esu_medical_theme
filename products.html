Our Products

Sustainable approach in healthcare.

Products

We provide connectors, cables and equipment used with or for surgeries - Connectors and Cables (Various Types), Arthroscopy and Orthopaedics, Vessel Sealers, Endoscopy Units, Power Tools, Rigid Scopes, Light Sources and Light Cables, Etc.

ESU Medical ’s business model is based on creating innovative products for existing equipment to enhance the product life cycle of equipment and surgical efficiency for modern day vessel sealer/RF generator (electrosurgery units. In order to reduce carbon footprint, we tend to focus on the 3R’s -

  1. Reuse - the device after sterilisation (as per recommendations).
  2. Recycle - the device/product in best possible way.
  3. Reduce - Wastage by preventing disposal of working equipment/devices thus extending their life cycle.

With a special focus on providing services to veterinary practises across UK and Europe, we stand out being a standalone company which provides ‘all in one’ solution for a hospital, clinic or independent practise regardless of make and model of a medical devise.
